## Research Focus

I work on **explainable** and **unsupervised anomaly detection**, with emphasis on reliable evaluation and actionable explanations for real-world systems.

**Current directions**
- **Evaluation frameworks** for unsupervised anomaly detection (see **[ASOI/ASI metric](https://github.com/jiyanMahmud/ASOI_ASI)**)
- **Explainability methods**: model-agnostic, model-specific, and **self-explainable** approaches
- **Graph-based propagation** and **root cause attribution** for multivariate anomalies
- **Language-based anomaly reasoning** and **log modeling**
- **User-facing XAI interfaces** and **real-time dashboards**

I collaborate on multiple **industry-funded projects with Deutsche Telekom Innovation Laboratories**, in partnership with ELTE (Budapest, Hungary).
